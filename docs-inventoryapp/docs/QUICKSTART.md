# Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with the OWPG Inventory Application in 5 minutes.

## For the Impatient

### What You'll Do Right Now

1. **Download** the app
2. **Set up** the server address
3. **Sign in** with your credentials
4. **Scan** a vehicle barcode
5. **Submit** your first checkout

**Estimated Time:** 5 minutes

---

## Step 1: Download & Install (1 min)

### Windows
- Download from your company portal or app store
- Run installer
- Grant permissions if asked
- Click "Launch"

### iPhone
- App Store > OWPG > Get > Install
- Wait for download
- Open app

### Android
- Google Play Store > OWPG > Install
- Wait for installation
- Open app

### Mac
- Download from company portal
- Drag to Applications folder
- Open Applications > OWPG

---

## Step 2: Configure Server (1 min)

When you open the app, you'll see the **IP Lookup** page.

**What to do:**
1. You'll see a text field asking for "Server IP Address"
2. Your administrator gave you an IP like `192.168.1.100` or a hostname
3. Type it in the field
4. Tap "Connect"

**That's it.** The app remembers this for future use.

---

## Step 3: Sign In (1 min)

After server setup, you'll see the **Sign In** page.

**What to do:**
1. Enter your **Username** (provided by your administrator)
2. Enter your **Password** (provided by your administrator)
3. Tap "Sign In"
4. Wait for authentication (5-10 seconds)
5. You'll be taken to the main page

---

## Step 4: Select Action & Scan Barcode (2 min)

You're now on the **Main Page** ready to do your first checkout.

**Choose Your Action:**

1. Locate the dropdown menu labeled "Select Reason For This Check Out"
2. Tap it
3. Choose the action you need:
   - **Test Drive** ← Most common
   - Maintenance
   - Relocation
   - Transfer
   - Service Test
   - Other

**Scan the Vehicle Barcode:**

1. Look for the **Camera icon** next to "Vehicle Beacon ID"
2. Tap the camera icon
3. Point your device at the vehicle's barcode (on windshield, door, or bumper)
4. Keep it steady, 6-12 inches away
5. When it detects (yellow highlight), the app auto-closes
6. The barcode is now entered

**Can't scan?** Manually type the barcode instead.

---

## Step 5: Complete the Form

**For Test Drive** (most common):

1. **Edit Customer Name** (tap the * button)
   - Enter customer's name
   - Tap Save

2. **Phone Number** - Enter their number

3. **Number of People** - How many passengers?

4. **Check the boxes:**
   - ☑ Valid Driver's License
   - ☑ Insurance Information
   - ☑ Picture/Video Access

5. **Tap Submit** at the bottom

6. **You'll see:** "Success! Checkout recorded"

**Done!** ✓

---

## Common Tasks

### Add a Customer (Test Drive)

1. Tap the **Edit Customer Name** button (the * button, looks red until filled)
2. A dialog opens
3. Type customer's first and last name
4. Tap "Save"
5. Button turns green ✓

### Take a Photo

1. Look for **Camera** or **Photo** button
2. Tap to open camera
3. Take photo (especially of vehicle exterior)
4. Review and confirm
5. Photo is saved with your checkout

### Try Manual Barcode Entry

1. Tap directly on the text field (not the camera)
2. Type or paste the barcode number
3. Should be 12+ characters
4. Tap outside field when done

### Sign Out

1. Look for **Sign Out** or **Logout** button (usually in menu)
2. Tap it
3. Confirm
4. You'll see the Sign In page
5. You can now sign in with different credentials

## If Something Goes Wrong

### Camera won't open
- Check Settings > App Permissions > Camera > Allow
- Restart the app

### Can't sign in
- Check username and password (case-sensitive password!)
- Ensure internet connection
- Contact administrator for password reset

### Can't connect to server
- Check the IP address (verify with administrator)
- Ensure WiFi/internet is connected
- Ask administrator if server is running

### Form won't submit
- Make sure all fields marked with * are filled
- Check internet connection
- Try again in 10 seconds

### Can't find vehicle barcode
- Check windshield (most common)
- Check door jamb
- Check bumper
- Ask supervisor for help

## What Happens Next

### During Test Drive
- Your device tracks location (if you allowed it)
- Vehicle location appears in real-time to your manager
- You continue about your day

### When Vehicle Returns
- Select "Maintenance" or "Other" action
- Scan the same barcode
- Add return details (odometer, condition)
- Take post-return photos
- Submit

### Your Checkout is Logged
- Records kept by company for accounting
- Photos and location saved
- Managers can see your activity
- Used for auditing and compliance

## Pro Tips

1. **Keep camera lens clean** - Barcode scans faster
2. **Have customer info ready** - Speeds up the form
3. **Take multiple photos** - Protects you if disputes arise
4. **Note the transaction ID** - For your records
5. **Sign out when done** - Keeps your account secure
6. **Check if barcode is damaged** - May need manual entry
7. **Ensure good lighting** - Makes scanning easier
8. **Test barcode scanner first** - Before you really need it
